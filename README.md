# 📊 Retail Sales Analytics: Customer Behavior and Revenue Insights

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover meaningful business insights related to customer behavior, product performance, sales trends, and profitability.

The project simulates a real-world end-to-end data analytics workflow, starting from raw data preprocessing and cleaning to generating actionable business insights and interactive visualizations for decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Clean and preprocess retail sales data for analysis.
* Identify sales trends and revenue drivers.
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior and segmentation.
* Evaluate product category performance and profitability.
* Support business decisions through data-driven insights and visualization.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

* **Home & Kitchen** products recorded higher return rates, negatively impacting profitability.
* **Personal Care** products generated stronger profit margins compared to other categories.
* **Online sales channels** underperformed relative to in-store sales, highlighting opportunities for digital growth.
* A relatively small group of customers contributed a significant portion of total revenue, indicating the importance of customer retention strategies.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Investigate return causes within the Home & Kitchen category.
* Expand marketing efforts for high-margin Personal Care products.
* Improve online customer experience and digital marketing initiatives.
* Develop loyalty programs targeting high-value customers.
